✅ Option Selling with Hedging Strategy

Option selling profitable ho sakta hai agar risk manage kiya jaye . Hedging se loss ka exposure control hota hai aur profit potential optimized rehta hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • Sell options to collect premium
  • Hedge position to limit losses
  • Objective: Risk-controlled premium collection

✅ Step by Step Setup

1️⃣ Identify Trend

  • Trend analysis using Moving Averages, Support/Resistance
  • Decide bullish/bearish bias

2️⃣ Option Selling Rules

  • Sell Call Option (Bearish view)
  • Sell Put Option (Bullish view)
  • Strike selection: ATM ya slightly OTM for maximum premium

3️⃣ Hedging Techniques

  • Buy a far OTM option in opposite direction
  • Example: Sell 100 CE, Buy 110 CE → Risk limited
  • Example: Sell 100 PE, Buy 90 PE → Risk limited

4️⃣ Profit & Loss Management

  • Max Profit: Collected premium
  • Max Loss: Difference between strikes - premium collected (if hedged)
  • Always maintain favorable Risk/Reward

✅ Always hedge when selling options to prevent unlimited losses.

Short-term time decay (Theta) works in favor of seller.

Monitor IV: High IV → better premium, Low IV → low premium.

Position sizing crucial to protect capital.
