✅ RSI Divergence Full Guide

RSI Divergence trading me ek powerful signal hai jo trend reversal ya weakness identify karta hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • RSI = Relative Strength Index (momentum indicator)
  • Divergence = Price aur RSI opposite direction me move karte hain
  • Objective: Trend reversal, trade timing improvement

✅ Types of RSI Divergence

1️⃣ Bullish Divergence (Buy Signal)

  • Price: Lower Low
  • RSI: Higher Low
  • 👉 Matlab: Price neeche ja raha hai, lekin momentum strength dikha raha hai → reversal ka signal

2️⃣ Bearish Divergence (Sell Signal)

  • Price: Higher High
  • RSI: Lower High
  • 👉 Matlab: Price upar ja raha hai, lekin momentum weak → potential downtrend

✅ Step by Step Identification

  • Chart me RSI indicator lagaye (Default: 14-period)
  • Price ka High/Low aur RSI ka High/Low dekho
  • Opposite movement → divergence signal
  • Support/Resistance ke paas divergence → strong signal
