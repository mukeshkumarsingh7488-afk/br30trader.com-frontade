✅ Intraday Trading Techniques

Intraday trading me same-day trades hoti hain jisme positions market close hone se pehle close karni hoti hain. Ye fast-paced aur high-risk hota hai, lekin proper techniques se profitable ban sakta hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • Trading ka objective: Short-term price movements ka profit lena
  • Positions open aur close same day
  • High liquidity aur volatility preferred

✅ Key Intraday Techniques

1️⃣ Trend Following

  • Market trend ke direction me trade karo
  • Tools: Moving Averages (MA), EMA, Supertrend
  • Example: Price above 20 EMA → Buy trend

2️⃣ Breakout Trading

  • Strong support/resistance cross hone par entry
  • Volume aur candle confirmation essential
  • Example: Resistance 18,000 cross → Long position

3️⃣ Pullback Strategy

  • Trend ke opposite temporary move par entry
  • Support/resistance aur Fibonacci retracement use karo
  • Example: Uptrend me minor dip → Buy

4️⃣ Scalping

  • Multiple small trades with quick entry/exit
  • Focus: 1-2% profit per trade
  • Requires fast execution and low spread

5️⃣ Momentum Trading

  • Stocks with high momentum aur news-based move
  • Entry: Strong price movement aur volume spike
  • Exit: Price slowdown ya target hit

6️⃣ Gap & Go Strategy

  • Pre-market price gap use karke trade
  • Entry: Open ke few minutes me momentum follow karo
  • Exit: Target/Stop loss based

✅ Intraday trading requires discipline & quick decisions ।

Always follow stop loss and risk management ।

Avoid overtrading aur emotional trading।

Focus on liquid stocks/indices for smooth execution।

Trading journal maintain karo → Mistakes aur learning track hota hai।
