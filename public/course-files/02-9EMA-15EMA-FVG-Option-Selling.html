✅ 9 EMA + 15 EMA + FVG (Option Selling) Strategy

Ye strategy short-term option selling ke liye EMA cross aur Fair Value Gap (FVG) ka combination use karti hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • EMA = Exponential Moving Average
  • FVG = Fair Value Gap → Price imbalance ka zone
  • Objective: Trend-following + High probability short trades

✅ Step by Step Setup

1️⃣ Chart Setup

  • Timeframe: 5-min / 15-min charts
  • Indicators: 9 EMA (Fast), 15 EMA (Slow)
  • Identify FVG: Last candle ka gap/jump jo price skip karta hai

2️⃣ Entry Rules (Option Selling)

Bearish Entry (Sell Call/Buy Put):

  • 9 EMA crosses below 15 EMA
  • Price retraces to FVG resistance
  • Sell Call Option near strike close to current price

Bullish Entry (Sell Put/Buy Call):

  • 9 EMA crosses above 15 EMA
  • Price retraces to FVG support
  • Sell Put Option near strike close to current price

✅ Exit Rules

  • Target: Next significant swing high/low
  • Stop Loss: Beyond FVG zone (for protection)
  • Risk management: Max 1-2% of capital per trade

✅ Important Notes

  • Confirm trend before entry using volume and candle pattern
  • EMA cross ke saath FVG zone ka confluence entry high probability banata hai
  • Risk/reward ka dhyan rakho, stop loss hamesha set kare
  • Multiple timeframes check karo (1H, 15-min) for confirmation
