✅ Scalping Techniques

Scalping trading ka maksad short-term me chhoti profits frequent trades se earn karna hai. Ye high-speed trading strategy hai aur zyada concentration aur discipline maangti hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • Scalping = Very short-term trading
  • Objective: Small price movements ka fayda uthana
  • Timeframe: 1 min, 5 min charts commonly use hote hain

✅ Scalping Strategies

1️⃣ Trend Scalping

  • Trend ko follow karo
  • Uptrend → Buy on pullbacks
  • Downtrend → Sell on rallies
  • Target: Few points per trade

2️⃣ Breakout Scalping

  • Support / Resistance break hone par enter
  • Volume confirmation zaruri
  • Stop loss tight rakho

3️⃣ Range Trading / Channel Scalping

  • Price range me oscillate kare
  • Buy near support, sell near resistance
  • Use oscillators like RSI / Stochastic

4️⃣ Momentum Scalping

  • High volume aur strong directional candle ke saath trade
  • Indicator: EMA crossover, MACD, Momentum
  • Exit: Jab momentum weaken ho

5️⃣ News Based Scalping

  • Market moving news par quick trades
  • Risk high → very tight stop loss
  • Use only major events
