✅ Trend Analysis Techniques

Trend analysis trading ka core concept hai. Market me trend follow karna aur trend reversal detect karna successful trading ke liye zaruri hai.

✅ Basic Concept

  • Trend = Market ka direction (Uptrend / Downtrend / Sideways)
  • Trend identify karke entry aur exit points optimize kiye ja sakte hain
  • Timeframes: Multiple timeframes जैसे 5 min, 15 min, 1H, Daily me trend analyze karo

✅ Key Trend Analysis Techniques

1️⃣ Moving Averages (MA)

  • Simple Moving Average (SMA), Exponential Moving Average (EMA)
  • Trend direction:
  • Price > MA → Uptrend
  • Price < MA → Downtrend
  • Crossovers:
  • Bullish crossover → Buy signal
  • Bearish crossover → Sell signal

2️⃣ Trendlines

  • Connect highs and lows to identify trend
  • Uptrend → Higher lows
  • Downtrend → Lower highs
  • Break of trendline → Potential reversal

3️⃣ Support & Resistance

  • Trend direction confirm karne ke liye levels use karo
  • Price breakout above resistance → Uptrend continuation
  • Price breakdown below support → Downtrend continuation

4️⃣ Price Action Patterns

  • Higher Highs (HH) aur Higher Lows (HL) → Uptrend
  • Lower Highs (LH) aur Lower Lows (LL) → Downtrend
  • Consolidation → Sideways trend

5️⃣ Indicators for Trend Confirmation

  • MACD → Trend direction aur strength
  • ADX → Trend ki strength measure
  • RSI → Overbought / Oversold confirmation
