✅ Option Buying Guide

Options trading me Option Buying सबसे common और beginner-friendly strategy है। इसमें Trader right buy karta hai बिना underlying को hold kiye, limited risk ke sath profit potential ke liye।

✅ Basic Concept

  • Option = Right to buy/sell underlying asset without obligation
  • Call Option: Buy → Price increase ka benefit
  • Put Option: Buy → Price decrease ka benefit
  • Risk limited to premium paid
  • Profit theoretically unlimited (call) ya limited (put)

✅ Step by Step Option Buying

1️⃣ Select the Option Type

  • Bullish → Call Option buy karo
  • Bearish → Put Option buy karo

2️⃣ Select Strike Price

  • ATM (At the Money) → Close to current price
  • ITM (In the Money) → Already profitable intrinsic value
  • OTM (Out of the Money) → Cheaper but higher risk

3️⃣ Select Expiry

  • Short-term → High gamma and theta decay
  • Medium-term → Balance risk-reward
  • Long-term → Lower theta decay

4️⃣ Check Greeks

  • Delta → Price sensitivity
  • Theta → Time decay (important for buyers)
  • Vega → Volatility impact
  • Gamma → Delta change rate

5️⃣ Entry & Exit

  • Entry → Market trend + breakout + confirmation candle
  • Exit → Profit target hit or stop loss
