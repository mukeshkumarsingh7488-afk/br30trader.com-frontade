✅ MACD Indicator Explained

MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) एक powerful trend और momentum indicator है।

✅ Basic Concept

  • MACD दो moving averages का difference है
  • Components:
  • 1️⃣ MACD Line = 12-period EMA – 26-period EMA
  • 2️⃣ Signal Line = 9-period EMA of MACD Line
  • 3️⃣ Histogram = MACD Line – Signal Line
  • Trend direction और strength पता करने में मदद करता है
  • Crossovers और divergence signals generate करता है

✅ MACD Signals

1️⃣ MACD Line Crosses Signal Line

  • Bullish Crossover: MACD Line ऊपर से Signal Line को cross करे → Buy Signal
  • Bearish Crossover: MACD Line नीचे से Signal Line को cross करे → Sell Signal

2️⃣ MACD Divergence

  • Price नए High बनाता है लेकिन MACD Lower High → Weakness → Sell Signal
  • Price नए Low बनाता है लेकिन MACD Higher Low → Weakness → Buy Signal

3️⃣ MACD Histogram

  • Histogram बढ़ रहा है → Trend strong
  • Histogram घट रहा है → Trend weak

✅ Step by Step MACD Trading

  1. Chart में MACD indicator add करो (Default: 12, 26, 9)
  2. Trend direction observe करो
  3. MACD line & Signal line crossover detect करो
  4. Divergence और histogram के साथ confirmation करो
  5. Entry और exit के लिए stop loss और target set करो

✅ Notebook Example Table

Signal Type Condition Action
Bullish Crossover MACD Line cross above Signal Line Buy / Long
Bearish Crossover MACD Line cross below Signal Line Sell / Short
Bullish Divergence Price Lower Low, MACD Higher Low Buy / Reversal
Bearish Divergence Price Higher High, MACD Lower High Sell / Reversal
Histogram Increase MACD - Signal Line widening Trend Strong
Histogram Decrease MACD - Signal Line narrowing Trend Weak
