✅ Candlestick Patterns Guide

Candlestick patterns market में price behavior समझने का आसान तरीका हैं।  
हर candle open, high, low, और close price दिखाती है।  

✅ Basic Candlestick Structure
- **Body:** Open aur Close price ke beech ka range  
- **Wicks/Shadows:** High aur Low price indicate करते हैं  
- **Color:**  
  - Green / White → Close > Open (Bullish)  
  - Red / Black → Close < Open (Bearish)  

✅ Important Patterns

1️⃣ **Doji**
- Open और Close price almost same  
- Signal: Market indecision / reversal potential  
- Types: Long-legged Doji, Dragonfly Doji, Gravestone Doji  

2️⃣ **Hammer & Hanging Man**
- Small body, long lower wick  
- Hammer → Bullish reversal (downtrend में)  
- Hanging Man → Bearish reversal (uptrend में)  

3️⃣ **Engulfing Pattern**
- Bullish Engulfing: Green candle fully engulfs previous red → Buy signal  
- Bearish Engulfing: Red candle fully engulfs previous green → Sell signal  

4️⃣ **Morning Star & Evening Star**
- Morning Star → Bullish reversal (downtrend में)  
- Evening Star → Bearish reversal (uptrend में)  

5️⃣ **Shooting Star**
- Small body, long upper wick  
- Uptrend में appear → Potential reversal (Sell Signal)  

✅ Step by Step Trading Setup
1️⃣ Chart select करो aur candlestick view on करो  
2️⃣ Trend और key support/resistance identify करो  
3️⃣ Candlestick patterns observe करो (Reversal / Continuation)  
4️⃣ Confirmation के लिए volume aur other indicators check करो  
5️⃣ Entry और exit points decide करो

✅ Important Notes
- Patterns ko standalone mat use karo, trend aur volume check करना जरूरी है  
- Higher timeframe patterns ज्यादा reliable होते हैं  
- Risk management hamesha follow karo (Stop Loss / Target)  
- Practice se pattern recognition fast aur accurate hoti hai
