✅ EMA Trading Strategy

EMA (Exponential Moving Average) एक popular trend-following indicator है जो recent price पर ज्यादा weight देता है।  

✅ Basic Concept
- EMA recent price action को ज़्यादा importance देता है  
- Fast EMA (e.g., 9-period) और Slow EMA (e.g., 21-period) use करके trend identify किया जाता है  
- Trend direction और crossover signals generate करता है  

✅ EMA Signals

1️⃣ **EMA Crossover**
- **Bullish Crossover**: Fast EMA (9) ऊपर से Slow EMA (21) को cross करे → Buy Signal  
- **Bearish Crossover**: Fast EMA (9) नीचे से Slow EMA (21) को cross करे → Sell Signal  

2️⃣ **Price above/below EMA**
- Price EMA के ऊपर → Uptrend  
- Price EMA के नीचे → Downtrend  

3️⃣ **EMA as Support/Resistance**
- EMA act करता है dynamic support/resistance की तरह  
- Bounce होने पर trend continuation का signal  

✅ Step by Step EMA Trading
1️⃣ Chart में Fast EMA और Slow EMA add करो (Default: 9, 21)  
2️⃣ Trend direction और crossover observe करो  
3️⃣ Price position के अनुसार entry/exit decision लो  
4️⃣ EMA bounce और confirmation candle pattern observe करो  
5️⃣ Stop loss और target set करो  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Signal Type           | Condition                                  | Action          |
|----------------------|--------------------------------------------|----------------|
| Bullish Crossover    | Fast EMA cross above Slow EMA             | Buy / Long     |
| Bearish Crossover    | Fast EMA cross below Slow EMA             | Sell / Short   |
| Price above EMA      | Price > EMA                               | Uptrend → Buy  |
| Price below EMA      | Price < EMA                               | Downtrend → Sell|
| EMA Bounce            | Price bounce from EMA support/resistance | Trend Continuation / Entry |

✅ Important Notes
- EMA best trend-following setup के लिए use करें  
- Multiple timeframe EMA observe करें → Signals strong  
- Candle confirmation और volume check करना जरूरी है  
- Risk management और stop loss हमेशा set करें
