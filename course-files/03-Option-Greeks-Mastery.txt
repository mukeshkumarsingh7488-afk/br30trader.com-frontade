✅ Option Greeks Mastery

Option Greeks trading ka backbone hain. Ye measure karte hain **option price ka sensitivity** underlying asset ke movement, time decay aur volatility ke respect me.  

✅ Key Greeks

1️⃣ **Delta (Δ)**
- Price sensitivity measure  
- 1 point move in underlying ka option price par impact  
- Call Delta: 0 to +1  
- Put Delta: 0 to -1  
- Example: Delta 0.6 ka matlab, 1 point price move → option price 0.6 point move karega  

2️⃣ **Gamma (Γ)**
- Delta ka rate of change measure  
- Price movement ke saath delta ka kitna change hoga  
- High Gamma → option price ka rapid change  

3️⃣ **Theta (Θ)**
- Time decay measure  
- 1 din ke liye option ka value kitna ghat raha hai  
- Long Call/Put: Negative Theta  
- Short Call/Put: Positive Theta  

4️⃣ **Vega (ν)**
- Volatility sensitivity measure  
- 1% change in IV → option price par impact  
- High Vega → option expensive/inexpensive with IV change  

5️⃣ **Rho (ρ)**
- Interest rate sensitivity measure  
- Rarely daily trading me use hota hai  
- Long Call: Positive Rho  
- Long Put: Negative Rho  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Greek   | Measures                   | Call Behavior | Put Behavior | Notes                          |
|--------|----------------------------|---------------|--------------|-------------------------------|
| Delta  | Price sensitivity          | +0 to +1      | 0 to -1      | ITM → Delta high               |
| Gamma  | Delta change rate          | Positive      | Positive     | ATM options high Gamma         |
| Theta  | Time decay                 | Negative      | Negative     | Short options positive Theta   |
| Vega   | Volatility sensitivity     | Positive      | Positive     | Long options high Vega         |
| Rho    | Interest rate sensitivity  | Positive      | Negative     | Rarely daily trading use       |

✅ Important Notes
- Greeks ka combination samajhna zaruri hai: Delta + Gamma + Theta + Vega  
- High Theta → beware of time decay  
- High Vega → watch IV before buying options  
- Use Greeks for **risk management, hedging, aur position sizing**
