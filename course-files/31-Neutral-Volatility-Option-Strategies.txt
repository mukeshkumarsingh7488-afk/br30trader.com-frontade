✅ Neutral / Volatility Option Strategies

Market me jab direction uncertain ho ya price range me rahe, tab ye strategies use hoti hain. Ye strategies mostly **premium decay** aur **volatility ka fayda** uthane ke liye design ki gayi hain.

✅ **Common Neutral / Volatility Strategies**

1️⃣ **Short Straddle**

* Sell 1 ATM Call + Sell 1 ATM Put at same strike
* Maximum profit agar price expiry me strike ke paas ho
* Loss unlimited agar price strongly upar ya neeche move kare
* Premium upfront milta hai

2️⃣ **Iron Butterfly**

* Sell 1 ATM Call + Sell 1 ATM Put + Buy 1 OTM Call + Buy 1 OTM Put
* Risk limited, reward limited
* Profit maximum agar price strike ke paas expire ho
* Volatility decay ka fayda

3️⃣ **Short Strangle**

* Sell 1 OTM Call + Sell 1 OTM Put
* Maximum profit agar price strikes ke beech rahe
* Loss unlimited agar price bahut move kare
* Premium collected upfront

4️⃣ **Short Iron Condor**

* Sell 1 OTM Put + Buy lower strike Put + Sell 1 OTM Call + Buy higher strike Call
* Risk limited
* Profit maximum agar price expected range me rahe
* Ideal for low volatility market

5️⃣ **Batman**

* Sell 1 ATM Put + Buy lower strike Put + Sell 1 ATM Call + Buy 2 higher strike Calls
* Risk limited, reward asymmetric
* Profit tab maximum jab price ATM ke paas ho
* Payoff shape kaafi unique, isliye “Batman”

6️⃣ **Double Plateau**

* Combination of double short strangle with protective wings
* Profit tab maximum in a specific price plateau
* Risk limited
* Works best in range-bound markets

7️⃣ **Jade Lizard**

* Sell 1 OTM Put + Sell 1 OTM Call + Buy higher strike Call
* No risk on upside (limited loss)
* Maximum profit from premium collected
* Neutral to slightly bullish strategy

8️⃣ **Reverse Jade Lizard**

* Sell 1 OTM Call + Sell 1 OTM Put + Buy lower strike Put
* No risk on downside
* Maximum profit from premium collected
* Neutral to slightly bearish strategy

✅ **Notes for Traders**

* Risk management zaruri hai
* Position sizing capital ke hisaab se adjust karein
* Entry/exit me technical indicators aur IV analysis ka use karein
* Har strategy ka max loss aur max profit samajhna zaruri hai
