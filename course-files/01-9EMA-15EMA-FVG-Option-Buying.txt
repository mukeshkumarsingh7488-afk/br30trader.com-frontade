✅ 9 EMA + 15 EMA + FVG (Option Buying) Strategy

Ye strategy **short-term option buying ke liye EMA cross aur Fair Value Gap (FVG) ka combination** use karti hai.  

✅ Basic Concept
- EMA = Exponential Moving Average  
- FVG = Fair Value Gap → Price imbalance ka zone  
- Objective: Trend-following + High probability entries  

✅ Step by Step Setup

1️⃣ **Chart Setup**
- Timeframe: 5-min / 15-min charts  
- Indicators: 9 EMA (Fast), 15 EMA (Slow)  
- Identify FVG: Last candle ka gap/jump jo price skip karta hai  

2️⃣ **Entry Rules (Option Buying)**
- Bullish Entry:  
  - 9 EMA crosses above 15 EMA  
  - Price retraces to FVG support  
  - Buy Call Option near strike close to current price  

- Bearish Entry:  
  - 9 EMA crosses below 15 EMA  
  - Price retraces to FVG resistance  
  - Buy Put Option near strike close to current price  

3️⃣ **Exit Rules**
- Target: Next significant swing high/low  
- Stop Loss: Below/Above FVG zone (for protection)  
- Risk management: Max 1-2% of capital per trade  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Condition        | EMA Setup                  | FVG Location          | Option Type      | Notes                    |
|-----------------|----------------------------|---------------------|----------------|-------------------------|
| Bullish Trend    | 9 EMA > 15 EMA             | FVG support zone     | Call Option     | Entry on pullback        |
| Bearish Trend    | 9 EMA < 15 EMA             | FVG resistance zone  | Put Option      | Entry on pullback        |

✅ Important Notes
- Confirm trend before entry using volume and candle pattern  
- EMA cross ke saath FVG zone ka confluence entry high probability banata hai  
- Risk/reward ka dhyan rakho, stop loss hamesha set kare  
- Multiple timeframes check karo (1H, 15-min) for confirmation
