✅ RSI & Stochastic Indicators

RSI और Stochastic indicators momentum aur market strength समझने के लिए सबसे popular tools हैं।  

✅ Relative Strength Index (RSI)
- RSI 0-100 range में होता है  
- Default period: 14  
- **Overbought:** RSI > 70 → Sell signal  
- **Oversold:** RSI < 30 → Buy signal  
- RSI divergence: Price और RSI अलग direction में → Trend reversal का signal  

✅ Step by Step RSI Trading
1️⃣ Chart में RSI indicator add करो (Period 14)  
2️⃣ Overbought/oversold levels observe करो  
3️⃣ Price divergence और candle pattern confirm करो  
4️⃣ Entry और exit points decide करो  

✅ Stochastic Oscillator
- %K और %D lines को track करता है  
- Scale: 0-100  
- **Overbought:** > 80 → Sell signal  
- **Oversold:** < 20 → Buy signal  
- Crossovers %K और %D में → Entry/Exit signals  

✅ Step by Step Stochastic Trading
1️⃣ Chart में Stochastic indicator add करो (Default: 14,3,3)  
2️⃣ Lines (%K & %D) observe करो  
3️⃣ Crossovers और overbought/oversold confirm करो  
4️⃣ Entry और exit points decide करो  

✅ Notebook में लिखने के लिए Example Table

| Indicator       | Condition                  | Signal          |
|-----------------|---------------------------|----------------|
| RSI             | >70 (Overbought)          | Sell           |
| RSI             | <30 (Oversold)            | Buy            |
| RSI Divergence  | Price ↓, RSI ↑ (Bullish) | Buy / Reversal |
| RSI Divergence  | Price ↑, RSI ↓ (Bearish) | Sell / Reversal|
| Stochastic      | %K cross above %D         | Buy            |
| Stochastic      | %K cross below %D         | Sell           |
| Stochastic      | >80 (Overbought)          | Sell           |
| Stochastic      | <20 (Oversold)            | Buy            |

✅ Important Notes
- Momentum indicators हमेशा trend के साथ use करो  
- RSI और Stochastic को combine करने से false signals कम होते हैं  
- Candle confirmation और volume analysis साथ में check करो  
- Multiple timeframe analysis reliable signals देता है
