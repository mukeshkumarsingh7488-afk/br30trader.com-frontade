✅ RSI Divergence Full Guide

RSI Divergence trading me ek powerful signal hai jo **trend reversal ya weakness** identify karta hai.  

✅ Basic Concept
- RSI = Relative Strength Index (momentum indicator)  
- Divergence = Price aur RSI opposite direction me move karte hain  
- Objective: Trend reversal, trade timing improvement  

✅ Types of RSI Divergence

1️⃣ **Bullish Divergence (Buy Signal)**
- Price: Lower Low  
- RSI: Higher Low  
👉 Matlab: Price neeche ja raha hai, lekin momentum strength dikha raha hai → reversal ka signal  

2️⃣ **Bearish Divergence (Sell Signal)**
- Price: Higher High  
- RSI: Lower High  
👉 Matlab: Price upar ja raha hai, lekin momentum weak → potential downtrend  

✅ Step by Step Identification
1. Chart me RSI indicator lagaye (Default: 14-period)  
2. Price ka High/Low aur RSI ka High/Low dekho  
3. Opposite movement → divergence signal  
4. Support/Resistance ke paas divergence → strong signal  

✅ Notebook Example Table

| Type             | Price Movement | RSI Movement  | Signal         |
|-----------------|----------------|---------------|----------------|
| Bullish Divergence | Lower Low      | Higher Low    | Buy / Reversal |
| Bearish Divergence | Higher High    | Lower High    | Sell / Reversal |

✅ Important Notes
- RSI Divergence ko standalone signal mat lo  
- Candle confirmation, volume confirmation bhi check karo  
- Higher timeframe (15-min, 1H) me signal zyada reliable  
- Stop Loss & Risk Management hamesha set karo
